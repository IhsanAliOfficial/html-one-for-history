SADDAM HUSSAIN

Saddam Hussein was an Iraqi politician and revolutionary who served as the fifth president of Iraq from 1979 until he was overthrown in 2003 during the U.S. invasion of Iraq.
He previously served as the vice president from 1968 to 1979 and also as the prime minister from 1979 to 1991 and later from 1994 to 2003. Wikipedia

Born: April 28, 1937, Al Awja, Iraq

Died: December 30, 2006 (age 69 years), Camp Justice

Movies: Long Days


Children: Uday Hussein, Qusay Hussein, Raghad Hussein, Rana Hussein, Hala Hussein, Ali Saddam Hussein al-Tikriti, Ali hussein

Spouse: Nidal al-Hamdani (m. 1990–2006), Samira Shahbandar (m. 1986–2006), Sajida Talfah (m. 1963–2006)

Education: Cairo University, University of Baghdad
